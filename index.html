Highlights

A considered user experience

We believe a great experience for your attendees should begin the moment they register for a ticket. That’s why we’ve designed our checkout flow to be as efficient as possible. That’s why we don’t support passing fees onto your attendees. And that’s why we are loved by the hundreds of thousands of customers who have used us to buy tickets.

Use your own website or CMS

We create an event page for you automatically, but with a couple of lines of code you can also embed your ticket form into your own website. We’re compatible with all the major Content Management Systems, and simply require a line of JavaScript and a line of HTML. The widget can be fully customised with CSS to match your site’s look.

No minimum fee

We believe our customers shouldn’t be punished for running events with low-priced tickets so we don’t have a minimum fee. We also believe those with high priced tickets shouldn’t be punished either, so we have a maximum fee per ticket too. Oh and we don’t charge for free events either. High five? High five.

Your data is your data

Data privacy is incredibly important to us. We do not use and we will not share your data, or your attendee data under any circumstances. This provides a robust, secure and compliant infrastructure for your events.
