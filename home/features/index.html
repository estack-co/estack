Everything you need, and nothing you don’t

All of these great features come as standard to help you run your best event yet

  • Control your money

    Connect your own Stripe or PayPal account to eStack and receive your money directly into your bank account: within 7 days for Stripe (2 days in the US and Australia), 3-4 days for PayPal. We don’t withhold your funds—in fact, we never see your money at all. .

  • Apple Pay built right in

    If you’re using Stripe as your payment gateway in one of Apple’s supported countries, your customers can pay instantly with Apple Pay. They don't need to fill in their card details so you can reduce incomplete payments and increase your ticket sales. Read more.

  • A gorgeous, responsive event homepage

    It’s ready to go straight away and it’s been designed to look and work great on mobile devices as well as the desktop. Easily customize it with your own logo, text, banner, links and Google Map locations. View example.

  • Add eStack to your own website

    If you want full control over the branding of your event homepage, you can use our fully embedded, fully stylable ticket form. All it takes is one line of JavaScript, and one line of HTML. Works seamlessly with any platform including Squarespace and WordPress. .

  • Discount codes

    Create flexible multi-use discount codes for promotional pricing, flash sales or create single use codes to hook up your friends with special rates. .

  • Secret tickets

    Easily create secret tickets that won't show up in the main ticket list. The ticket will only be accessible from a secret url that you control. Perfect for speaker tickets, volunteers, or attendees with special pricing. .

  • Donation tickets

    Allow attendees to pay what they like for their ticket. The is really popular with community and charity events. Of course, you can set a minimum amount so you won't be out of pocket.

  • Activities

    You no longer have to worry about selling out of ‘Saturday Only’ tickets because you sold too many ‘Weekend’ tickets. Activities let you manage attendees for each ‘activity’ separately to your ticket sales. It's very powerful, read more about activities .

  • Collect attendee data

    Collect information like dietary requirements, or t-shirt sizes from your attendees during the ticket purchasing process. View the answers in-app or download the results to process them offline. .

  • Check-in apps

    Effortlessly check-in your attendees on-site with our suite of tools. Either use our device-agnostic web-based check-in tool, or use our iOS or Android QR code scanner. .

  • Teams for event administration

    Invite others to join your team and help manage your event. You have full control over who can access financials, create new events and check-in attendees.

  • Messages

    We’ve added the ability to message your existing attendees, attendees from a previous event, and your mailing lists. If you wish you can provide your own HTML templates, and then use variables to include dynamic content. .

  • Waiting Lists

    Collect names and email addresses once a ticket has sold out. You can then message people on the list and offering them a ticket, with an optional expiry date. .

  • Internationalisation (private beta)

    We are beginning to add multilingual support to the Tito checkout flow. At the moment, we have the following languages available: Spanish, French and Polish. Drop us an email if you’re interested.

  • Full data export

    At any time you can download a full csv or Excel export of event data, including customer data, purchase details and questionnaire responses that have been collected.

  • Custom domains

    You have full control over your url when setting up accounts and events: http://host.estack.co/o/[org-id] & http://host.estack.co/e/[event-id] . eStack also allows you to fully customize by using your own private domain url.
